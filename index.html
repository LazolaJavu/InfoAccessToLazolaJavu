ABOUT ME

I started working on mathematical models and data analysis while doing my Honours and continued to my Master's degree. That's where my interest in data science came to be. Ever since then, I haven't been able to look back.

I earned my undergraduate degree at the University of Fort Hare in 2015. After that, I went to the University of Johannesburg and obtained an Honours degree in Physics, where I majored in Astrophysics and earned it in 2017. My project identifying Seyferts galaxies gave me my first introduction to mathematical modelling. I earned my Master's in Physics at the University of Johannesburg in 2021. I focused on evaluating selected clear-sky models' performance in SA conditions and identifying suitable ones.

I was fortunate to enrol at Explore AI (formerly known as Explore Data Science Academy) to learn data science techniques applied to the real world. Currently, I'm working as a Junior Data Scientist at an NGO.
